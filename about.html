What are Red Pandas?:

The red panda is an elusive and mysterious mammal of the biodiverse Himalayan forests. They have also been called the "firefox", “lesser panda”, and “red-cat-bear”— the red panda has many nicknames.

What's Special about Red Pandas?:

  1. There are less than 10,000 red pandas left in the wild
  2. Almost 50% of the red panda’s habitat is in the Eastern Himalayas
  3. Giant pandas are named after red pandas--red pandas are the original pandas!
  4. Red pandas are the only living member of their own taxonomic family
